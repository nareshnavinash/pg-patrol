How It Works

Three layers of protection, all running locally on your device.

🤡

Bad Words → Funny Words

Profanity is replaced with hilarious, family-friendly alternatives in real-time. Pages stay readable and natural.

What the f*** → What the fudge
🐾

NSFW Images → Cute Animals

Explicit images are detected by on-device AI and silently replaced with adorable animals and tasty food photos.

[explicit image] → 🐶 🦀 🍕
🌞

Bad News → Gentle Overlay

Good Vibes Mode softens distressing content like graphic news and toxic discussions with a calming overlay.

[violent headline] → Content softened 💜

Feature Highlights

Everything you need to keep browsing family-friendly.

⚡

Real-Time Protection

Works on dynamic pages: social feeds, infinite scroll, lazy-loaded images, and live comment sections.

🧠

ML Text Toxicity

MiniLM-based transformer model detects toxic text beyond simple keyword matching.

📝

Custom Word Lists

Add your own blocked words and safe words. You're in full control of what gets filtered.

🌐

Site Whitelisting

Trust specific websites and disable filtering where you don't need it.

🎧

Sensitivity Levels

Three levels — Mild, Moderate, Strict — to match your family's comfort.

📈

Activity Log & Stats

See exactly what was filtered. Track all-time stats across your browsing.

👁

Reveal Toggle

Temporarily view original content when you need to. One click to reveal, one click to re-hide.

📷

NSFW Image AI

ViT-Tiny ONNX model with multi-crop inference detects explicit images without any cloud calls.

🔒

100% On-Device. Zero Data Sent to Servers.

Both AI models run entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your browsing data never leaves your device. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud APIs.
