PG Patrol: Free Parental Control Extension — Profanity Filter & NSFW Image Blocker

PG Patrol is a free, open-source parental control browser extension and web content filter that makes the internet safer for families. It works as a profanity filter, NSFW image blocker, and safe browsing tool — all powered by on-device AI. Available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, Opera, and Arc.

ALL FILTERING HAPPENS LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE. No data is ever sent to external servers.

━━━ CORE FEATURES ━━━

★ Profanity Filter
Replaces swear words and offensive language with funny, family-friendly alternatives. Pages stay readable and natural.

★ NSFW Image Blocker
Detects and hides explicit or sexual images using a local AI model. Blocked images are silently replaced with pleasant stock photos (cute animals, food) — no obvious banners, no curiosity spikes. The page layout stays intact.

★ Good Vibes Mode
Softens distress-heavy content blocks (graphic news, toxic discussions) with a gentle overlay, creating a calmer browsing experience.

★ Real-Time Protection
Works on dynamic pages: social feeds, infinite scroll, lazy-loaded images, and comment sections. Protection continues as new content appears.

━━━ CUSTOMIZATION ━━━

• Three sensitivity levels: Mild, Moderate, Strict
• Add your own blocked words and safe words
• Whitelist trusted websites
• Per-page activity log shows what was filtered
• All-time stats tracking
• Reveal toggle to temporarily view original content

━━━ AI & PRIVACY ━━━

PG Patrol uses two local AI models:
• Text toxicity detection (MiniLM-based transformer, 6-label Jigsaw model)
• Image NSFW detection (ViT-Tiny ONNX model with multi-crop inference)

Both models run entirely on your device using WebAssembly. No cloud APIs required.
• On Chrome 131+, borderline text is also refined by Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano — fully local, zero cost

Optional: Connect your own Google Perspective API key for enhanced text analysis. This is completely opt-in and disabled by default.

━━━ PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED ━━━

• storage — saves your settings and preferences
• activeTab — accesses the current tab for filtering
• alarms — schedules periodic word list updates
• offscreen — runs AI models in an isolated background context (Chrome only)
• host_permissions (all URLs) — injects content scripts to filter pages you visit

━━━ IMPORTANT NOTE ━━━

No filter is perfect. PG Patrol significantly reduces unwanted exposure but some content may occasionally slip through or be over-filtered. Adjust sensitivity and custom word lists for the best experience.

Open source and community-driven.
